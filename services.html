The importance of tax compliance for your business

Taxes are a legal requirement for all citizens, including businesses operating inside acountry's borders. In terms of corporate tax compliance, some of the advantagesinclude the company's ability to operate legally. With this legality, a business unit'scredibility rises, followed by the structure of the company's financial projections inthe future. This has an impact on your firm if you wish to request for a bank loan (upto 60 million Rupiah) to help you grow your business.

We help in calculating, paying, and reporting your company's monthly and annual tax returns

Tax compliance services will be provided by RED to assist clients in preparing anddelivering the duty of taxpayers to comply with their taxes obligations in a timelyand accurate way. The following are examples of compliance services:

Monthly Tax Return:

  1. VAT (Value Added Tax)
  2. PPh Art. 4 (2) Final (Rentals on Land and / or Buildings)
  3. PPh Art. 4 (2) Final (Certain Gross Turnover)
  4. PPh Art. 15 (Shipping & Flight)
  5. PPh Art. 21 & 26 (Employee Salary / personal income)
  6. PPh Art. 22 (Imported Goods, Customs etc.)
  7. PPh Art. 23 & 26 (Tax on Services)
  8. PPh Art. 25 (Company)

Annual Tax Return:

  1. Ending period December PPh. Art. 21 & 26 (Form 1721)
  2. Annual Tax Return of Individual Taxpayers (Form 1770, Form 1770S, Form1770SS)
  3. Annual Tax Return of Corporate Taxpayers (Form 1771)
  • Annual and monthly corporate income tax returns
  • Annual and monthly employee (payroll) income tax returns
  • Monthly withholding tax returns
  • Monthly VAT returns
